If you wanna check out some of my projects:

  • U-Reach: If you're a college student in Georgia, this is the webapp for you. U-Reach allows you to connect with other students on your campus who share the same interests as you. Reach us at u-reach.appspot.com.

  • Vote it, poll it!: Made during a very political season, this webapp was made to make it easier for people to be involved in our democracy. During election season, you'll get a rundown of what's on your ballot and resources to help you get to know each candidate. Check it out at vote-it-poll-it.appspot.com.

  • PlayShare: I always say PlayShare is my brainchild because it was the first project I designed, coded, and built all by myself. If you love music, but you're sick of hearing the same old playlist, PlayShare will allow you to create random playlists based around a theme. You can also share your playlists, so that others can see what you're listening to. Music sharing is at your fingertips at my-playshare.appspot.com.
